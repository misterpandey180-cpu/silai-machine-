About Our Training Center

Our Silai Machine Operator Training Center provides professional training for sewing machine operation and garment production. We focus on practical skills, modern stitching techniques, and industry standards to help students build careers in garment factories, tailoring shops, and fashion industries.

Popular Training Programs

Basic Sewing Machine Operation

Learn how to operate different types of sewing machines safely.

Garment Stitching Techniques

Training in stitching shirts, pants, and other garments.

Fabric Cutting & Measurement

Learn proper measurement and fabric cutting techniques.

Quality Control & Finishing

Learn how to check garment quality and finishing methods.

Why Choose Us?

We provide high-quality training with real-world garment production practice. Our goal is to develop skilled sewing machine operators who can work efficiently in garment factories, tailoring units, and fashion industries.
